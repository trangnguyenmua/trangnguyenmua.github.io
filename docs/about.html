Trang Nguyen is a Vietnamese-Chinese American makeup, nail, and hair artist based in New York City.

Born and raised in the District of Columbia, she was able to enjoy the many diverse cultures and communities around her. After sneaking into drag shows as a teenager, Trang found her calling as a makeup artist; inspired by the glamour, glitter, and music.

Trang established herself as a manicurist and esthetician in 2012. After 5 years in the Baltimore area, Trang relocated to New York in 2017 to pursue makeup fulltime. With over 8 years in the beauty industry, she has worked with brands ranging from Canon to Bobbi Brown, and is known for mixing natural tones with added pops of vibrant color.
